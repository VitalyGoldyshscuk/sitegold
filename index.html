About Me

Concept designer, matte painter and illustrator from Lviv. I've been working in the entertainment industry for over 6 years doing everything from character and environment design to marketing illustration.
Currently I'm employed by Ubisoft Redlynx as a Concept Artist.
If you have any questions about my work or wish to commission artwork contact me.
